_By Parliamentary Opposition Leader, DAP Secretary-General and MP for Tanjung, Lim Kit Siang, in Penang on Friday, 6th August , 1993:_

# Kit Siang will explain DAP stand on the Ghafar-Anwar UMNO ‘Battle of the Decade’ at the Penang DAP Ceramah at Penang Chinese Town Hall tomorrow night

I will explain DAP stand on the Ghafar-Anwar UMNO‘Battle of the Decade’ at the Penang DAP Ceramah at Penang Chinese Town Hall tomorrow ( Saturday) night.</u>

Other issues which I would talk about in tomorrow’s Penang Chinese Town Hall ceramah include:

	DAP’s emergency motion in Parliament on the position og Ghafar Baba as Deputy Prime Minister;

	The parliamentary controversary with Anwar Ibrahim in connection with the RM6.27 billion national sewerage services privatisation;

	The latest Constitution Amendment Bill which violated the democratic rights of the people;

	The politics of the stock-market and the Union Paper Holdings Bhd. Shares scandal;

	The three motives of MCA President, Datuk Dr. Ling Liong Sik in trying to question the DAP Save Bukit China One-Person Ome-Dollar Fund;

	MCA’s RM20 million Langkawi Project;

	The MAIKA Telekom shares hijacking scandal of MIC President, Datuk Seri S. Samy Vellu;

	DAP’s firm and uncompromising opposition to any infringement by PAS Kelantan Government of non-Muslim rights and sensitivities, as in the recent regulations restricting public sale and consumption of alcohol affecting non-Muslim; and

	Why it is important that every Malaysian above 21 years should register as voters by August 25.
